Archive of our Own - Haikyuu Fanwork Data Visualisation

The "Archive of Our Own" (AO3), a nonprofit open source repository for fanworks, has become one of the most popular archives for transformative works since its founding in 2008.

Haikyuu!! (ハイキュー!!) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. On this page I have collected several statistics about the fanfictions of the Haikyuu!! series. This was achieved by going through the tag "Haikyuu!!" on AO3. This means that any work not tagged with this was not considered. Works that are only viewable when logged in are also not considered as they cannot be saved with my script.
